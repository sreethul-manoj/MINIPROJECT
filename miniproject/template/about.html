About CivicEYE

CivicEYE is a public complaint registration portal designed to empower citizens to report issues directly to the authorities.

Users can easily register complaints through the platform by providing details and uploading relevant images. Each complaint is securely stored and made accessible to the admin team for review and action.

Key Features:

  • User-friendly complaint registration form
  • Option to upload complaint-related images
  • Admin dashboard to view and manage all complaints
  • Status tracking and timely updates

Our mission is to create a transparent, efficient, and accountable system for addressing public issues — from potholes and garbage to illegal activities and safety concerns.
